# Account Deletion Policy Edubadges

`Last update: 23 december 2025`

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains how users can delete their account and what data will be deleted in accordance with the GDPR and Google Play requirements.

## 2. Deleting an account

Users can delete their account at any time via:

* The account settings in the app, or
* By sending a request to [support@edubadges.nl](mailto:support@edubadges.nl).

After the request, the account will be deactivated immediately and permanently deleted within 30 days.

## 3. Deleted data

Upon deletion, the following data will be deleted:

* Personal information (name, email address, telephone number, profile information)
* Login details
* Settings and preferences
* Any documents or uploads linked to the account

Not deleted: data that must be retained by law (e.g. invoices) or anonymised statistics.

## 4. Backups

Data in backups is automatically deleted once the backup rotation is complete (maximum 30 days).

## 5. Recovery

Deleted accounts and data cannot be recovered once the deletion is complete.

## 6. Contact

Questions about account deletion can be sent to: [support@edubadges.nl](mailto:support@edubadges.nl)
